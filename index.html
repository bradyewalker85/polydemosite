The Science

Setting the Standard for Day-One Accuracy.

The Challenge

Most CGMs face a "Day One Gap" where the filament needs 12–24 hours to equilibrate with interstitial fluid — leaving patients without reliable data during the most critical window.

The Metric Solution

Proprietary Hydro-Sonic™ Calibration — a gravity-fed reagent titration ritual performed at the manufacturing stage.

The Process

Sensors are placed in a gravity-fed chamber where a temperature-controlled synthetic reagent is filtered through the assembly — washing the filament and pre-hydrating the enzyme layer.

The Clinical Result

First-2-hour MARD reduced by 34% compared to dry-shipped competitors. Full accuracy from the moment of application.

Support

You Are Never Out of Range.

Every error code has a clear Why and a clear Fix. No alarms — only actionable guidance.

E-102 Pressure Low

A "Compression Low" caused by sleeping directly on the sensor site, momentarily occluding interstitial fluid flow.

Fix: Roll off the sensor site. Readings normalize within 15 minutes.

E-404 Adhesive Shear

Mechanical trauma from snagging the device — typically on clothing or equipment — causing adhesive layer separation.

Fix: Request a "Shear Replacement" via the Metric App under our 14-day guarantee.

E-001 Signal Loss

The Metric App cannot establish a BLE 5.2 connection. The phone is likely more than 20 feet from The Core™ transmitter.

Fix: Move closer to the sensor site and wait 5 minutes for automatic re-pairing. No data is lost — The Core™ onboard buffer retains up to 7 days of readings.

Acetaminophen Interaction

The Metric Fiber™ enzyme matrix is chemically engineered to be Acetaminophen-insensitive. Tylenol will not falsely raise or interfere with glucose readings.
