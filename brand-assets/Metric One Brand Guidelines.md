\# Metric Medical Systems: Visual Brand Guidelines

\#\# 1\. Brand Essence  
\* \*\*Mission:\*\* Precision for Every Body. Clarity for Every Life.  
\* \*\*Positioning:\*\* "The Concierge of Clinical Data." Bridging high-end retail with life-saving medical accuracy.  
\* \*\*Tone:\*\* Precise, Calm, Transparent, and Action-Oriented.

\#\# 2\. Visual Identity

| Element | Hex Code | Usage |

\#\#\# Typography  
\* \*\*Headlines:\*\* Inter (Bold/Extra Bold) | \-2% Tracking.  
\* \*\*Body/Data:\*\* IBM Plex Sans (Regular/Medium) | 140% Leading.

\#\# 3\. The Hydro-Sonic™ Ritual (Visual Standard)  
\*The "Pour-Over" sequence in video assets must adhere to these standards:\*  
\* \*\*Framing:\*\* High-contrast, macro-cinematography.  
\* \*\*Speed:\*\* 60fps (smooth, deliberate movement).  
\* \*\*Color Shift:\*\* Lighting should shift to "Cool Blue" upon liquid contact with the sensor.  
\* \*\*Sound:\*\* Glass-on-glass clicks, high-frequency "shimmer" tones. No cafe/ambient noise.

\#\# 4\. Imagery & Facility Standards  
\* \*\*The Metric Hub:\*\* Bright, shadowless illumination. Materials: Frosted glass, light oak, matte white surfaces.  
\* \*\*The Agent:\*\* Professionals wear charcoal-grey, collarless technical tunics (Metric Hub Liaisons).  
\* \*\*Macro Shots:\*\* Focus on the \*\*Metric Fiber™\*\* and the blue LED activation light on the transmitter.

\#\# 5\. The Agent Persona (AI Guidelines)  
\* \*\*Rule 1:\*\* Never guess. Use "Verification Holds" for unknown data.  
\* \*\*Rule 2:\*\* Use clinical terminology (e.g., "Interstitual Fluid," "Sensor Site").  
\* \*\*Rule 3:\*\* Proactive Reassurance. Always provide the "Why" and "Fix" for system errors.
